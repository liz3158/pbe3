Sweetness



I have a desire for sweets, I keep a bag handy
love my sweets, eat all kinds of candy
my favorite sweets during each season varies
this season my favorite is chocolate covered cherries

Love to bite into that sugary delight
the liquid squirts out with every bite
that creamy coating is simply grand
I like to keep a box in my left hand

I like to throw them up in the air
then catch them in my mouth without a care
can taste them now, think I’ll get my box out
they are sweet and that’s without doubt

After I eat about three, my belly says whoa
too much sugar and to the bathroom I go
been eating chocolate covered cherries for fifty years
but I will be changing to pop cycles as Spring nears

By Liz Wong Wan Qi
